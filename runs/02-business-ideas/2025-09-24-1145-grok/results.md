## TLDR Summary

Top ideas focus on SaaS tools for developers (e.g., code review, boilerplates) due to high feasibility for solo devs, strong profitability via subscriptions, growing demand in tech, and perfect skill alignment. Games offer passion projects but lower profitability; services provide quick revenue but less scalability. Prioritize SaaS for sustainable online businesses.
